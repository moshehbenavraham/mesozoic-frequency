# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Curious adults, students, parents, and creative technologists opening a link on mobile or desktop to be surprised by a dinosaur-themed web experience rather than a conventional article.

## Product Purpose

Create a memorable, publishable single-page visual experience about dinosaurs that demonstrates craft: interactive time depth, cinematic paleo mood, factual touchpoints, and responsive polish. Success means the page feels authored, immersive, and immediately shareable.

## Brand Personality

Nocturnal museum, fossil-radar, volcanic calm.

## Anti-references

Avoid cartoon jungle tropes, Jurassic Park imitation, generic green/brown educational worksheets, neon cyber dashboards, stock-photo hero templates, identical card grids, gradient text, and AI-looking purple/indigo landing-page scaffolds.

## Design Principles

1. Make deep time tactile: visitors should feel millions of years as a manipulable surface.
2. Treat dinosaurs as specimens, not mascots: elegant, strange, and materially present.
3. Let interaction reveal information instead of dumping prose.
4. Use color as atmosphere: blue-hour fossil lab with mineral amber and volcanic rust.
5. Preserve readability and reduced-motion access even when the page gets theatrical.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast or better for body text. Support keyboard interaction on all controls, meaningful labels, responsive layout for small screens, and a reduced-motion mode that removes large transitions while preserving content.
